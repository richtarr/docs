This is where you can manage individual time tracking entries. You can see which volunteer created the entry, and modify the sign in and out times.

## Missed Sign Outs

If a volunteer forgets to sign out, they will automatically be signed out at midnight on the same day.

When an entry is automatically signed out, it will be flagged in the list of entries:

You can also click the filter in the top right corner to show only the entries that were automatically signed out:

To resolve a missed sign out, click the 'resolve' button next to the entry. This will allow you to edit the sign out time, and remove the flag from the entry.
